# Privacy Policy for NameDrill

**Last updated: February 6, 2025**

## Overview

NameDrill ("the App") is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We believe your personal data — especially photos of people you know — should stay completely under your control.

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored Locally (On Your Device Only)
- **Photos**: All photos you add to the app are stored locally on your device
- **Names and notes**: Information you enter about people
- **Learning progress**: Your practice history and spaced repetition data
- **App settings**: Your preferences (dark mode, notification settings, etc.)

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not upload your photos to any server
- We do not collect personal information
- We do not track your location
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not use analytics that identify you personally

## Third-Party Services

### RevenueCat (In-App Purchases)
If you choose to make a purchase, we use RevenueCat to process transactions. RevenueCat receives only the information necessary to complete your purchase (handled through Apple App Store or Google Play Store). We do not receive or store your payment information.

For RevenueCat's privacy practices, see: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### App Store / Play Store
Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play Store. These platforms have their own privacy policies.

## Permissions

### Camera (Optional)
Used only to take photos of people you want to add to the app. Photos are saved locally and never uploaded.

### Photo Library (Optional)
Used only to import existing photos into the app. Photos remain on your device.

### Notifications (Optional)
Used only to send you practice reminders at times you choose. Notification content is generated locally.

## Data Storage & Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS/Android secure storage. We do not have access to your data.

If you uninstall the app, all data is deleted from your device.

## Data Export & Deletion

- **Export**: You can export your data at any time from Settings > Export Data
- **Delete**: You can delete all data from Settings > Delete All Data
- **Uninstall**: Uninstalling the app removes all associated data

## Children's Privacy

NameDrill does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences (teachers, professionals, etc.).

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
