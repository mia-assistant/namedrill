# Screenshot Concepts for App Store & Play Store

## General Guidelines

- **Dimensions:** 
  - iPhone: 1290 x 2796 px (6.7") or 1242 x 2688 px (6.5")
  - Android: 1080 x 1920 px minimum, 16:9 aspect ratio recommended
- **Style:** Clean device frames, consistent background color (suggest brand purple/blue gradient or soft white)
- **Text:** Large, bold headlines above each screen
- **Diversity:** Use diverse placeholder faces in screenshots (age, ethnicity, gender)

## Notes for Designer

- Keep consistent visual language across all screenshots
- Use device frames (iPhone 15 Pro, Pixel 8) for professional look
- Background: Consider soft gradient or solid color that matches app theme
- Typography: Bold sans-serif for headlines (SF Pro Display or Roboto)
- Ensure text is readable at small sizes (app store thumbnails)
